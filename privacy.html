Information we collect

Make Color! is a lightweight browser game. We may process gameplay-related information such as session identifiers, submitted scores, selected mode, elapsed time, and technical security logs used to reduce abuse and cheating.

Google AdSense and cookies

This site may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver, measure, and improve ads. You can manage ad personalization through your Google ad settings and browser cookie controls.

How information is used

Gameplay and log data is used to operate the site, keep leaderboards and score submission fair, investigate technical errors, and maintain a stable experience for visitors.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Advertising-related processing may be handled by Google and its partners according to their own privacy policies.
